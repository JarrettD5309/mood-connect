MoodConnect is a Mood Media app that connects you to how you are feeling and others.

How Does MoodConnect Work?

Select your emotion

MoodConnect asks the question "How are you feeling?" To answer this question, we offer primary and secondary mood options. Your selection informs our AI of what resources or connections will accommodate your needs.

Connect!

Once you select your emotion, you will have the option to connect with another user to have an anonymous audio or text based chat. If you're not ready to connect with another user yet, that's o.k. We also have lots of great resource - such as guided meditations, breathing exercises, yoga classes, articles, and more for you to access.

Feel Better

After a person completes a connection or activity from the resource library, MoodConnect will ask the user: "How Are you Feeling Now?" MoodConnect's primary purpose is to help people feel better and live healthy and productive lives.
