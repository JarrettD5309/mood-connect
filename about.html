About Us

Our Mission
Our Mission

The mission of MoodConnect LLC is to help users to connect more deeply with their own feelings and to assist users in being more invested in their own physical and mental health and wellness.

MoodConnect also wants to help to reduce suicide and help users who are experiencing ideations of suicide by providing resources that will immediately send them the help that they need.

If you would like to learn more about the founder and CEO of MoodConnect, visit Our Founder's page.
