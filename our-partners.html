Our Partners & Affiliates

Jivamukti Yoga

As a path to enlightenment through compassion for all beings, Jivamukti Yoga is grounded in the original meaning of the Sanskrit word asana as “seat, connection” – relationship to the Earth. Earth implies all of life.

Rise Science

Rise is a mobile app that uses data from your phone and/or wearable and biomathematical models from sleep science to learn your unique sleep biology. These models are widely accepted by researchers in the sleep science field, which is nearly 100 years old.

Urgent Care for Mental Health

The Urgent Care for Mental Health program takes a unique approach to providing mental health care in our community. The providers simply run their practice as they always have and see the patients the program generates. In exchange for getting these referrals they agree to reserve several appointments per day to see Urgent Care for Mental Health patients.
