Our Founder

Amy Ouzoonian

For most of her life Amy Ouzoonian has been helping others to reach their highest potential. She enjoys networking with people of all backgrounds, economic levels and interests. She has enjoyed being a bridge to connect people in the yoga, marketing and art communities in New York City, Arizona, California, New Orleans, Louisiana, and beyond. As a yoga teacher, single mother and member of various groups and communities, Amy has seen the advantages of working with others, and of being an individual and having time to get introspective and work on herself to be a better person.

In early May of 2020 Amy was laid off from her job due to the COVID-19 pandemic and was struggling with so many others to find peace and serenity. She awoke from her state of delving deep within and conceived the name and core concept that is MoodConnect, the only web application that allows users to identify and explore how they are feeling and connect with others based on how they are feeling.

Amy Ouzoonian is the creator of Mood Media™ which is a user emotion driven resource. She is also the CEO and founder of MoodConnect™, a mood media app that allows users to connect with each other based on how they are feeling.
